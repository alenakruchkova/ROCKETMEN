About Rocketmen

Have you ever wondered how many people are in space right now? Who are they? How long have they been out there?

Rocketmen provides users with realtime information about astronauts. Keep tabs on who's floating, what they are like and who's been in space the longest. Follow them as they work and explore in zero gravity. Share their photos and videos via twitter. Be notified when the number of people in space changes.

Rocketmen makes it easy for you to spot the International Space Station (ISS) and watch it on a map as it moves across the globe. You can also request a forecast of the next visible pass for any location in the world.

This is an open source project. We welcome any and all suggestions and contributions!
